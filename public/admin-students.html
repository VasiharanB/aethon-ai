How to talk to Aethon Intelligence

You can ask Aethon to fetch, filter, and analyze any data from the platform. Try formats like these:

  • Counting: "How many students are registered?" or "How many assessments do we have?"

  • Security: "Find all students who have a 'Critical' severity proctoring violation."
